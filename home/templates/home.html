WHO WE ARE

Montage Studio was created by a group of young engineering students of RUET and RU, they chose their passion as their profession and this incident of their life have been pushing them constantly to expand their potentiality.

Right now Montage Studio has one of the highest number of fan following among Bangladeshi wedding photography Facebook pages based in Rajshahi. Among many other achievements we recognize this as the most important one as it directly indicates how many people are relating with us personally. Their likes and comments inspire us to do better every day.

In the last 3 years we have covered more than 100+ events. Our team is a small but strong team of 4 Photographers, cinematographers & Editors. All our photographers are highly educated, most of them are engineers. Recently we have taken Montage Studio to an countrywide level.
